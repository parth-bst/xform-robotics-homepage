Technical Specifications

Physical Design

  • Weight: 3kg (6.6 lbs)
  • Locomotion: 4-wheeled autonomous robot - 3 inch dia big wheels for versatile flat surfaces. Tackles carpets, and small floor height differences with a breeze
  • Surface Compatibility: Smooth surfaces and carpets
  • Materials: Lightweight construction for home mobility

AI & Computing

  • Natural Language: Pure natural language conversation
  • Local LLMs: For basic in-house mobility and mimicking in pet sounds
  • Memory: Long-term and short-term memory systems
  • Context Awareness: Schedule your reminders. It also identifies your habits and may help you build new ones

Sensors & Vision

  • Cameras: 1 HD 5-8MP camera
  • Stereo Vision: 2 stereo vision cameras
  • Sensors: Touch sensor and proximity sensor
  • Natural Environment: Continuous contextual awareness

Power & Battery

  • Battery Life: 2 hours peak usage (more for regular life)
  • Charging: Charging mat for reliable 24x7 operations
  • Auto Charging: Built for continuous operation
  • Power Management: Optimized for home environment
